La Casita Center — Louisville, KY
Source: https://www.lacasitacenter.org/ (scraped 2026-06-13)

ABOUT
La Casita Center is a grassroots non-profit located in Louisville, KY that accompanies
families in the Latinx community. Founded in 2005 by a small group of social workers,
teachers, mothers, and members of religious communities who recognized a growing need
for community building and access to services, education, and support in the Latinx
community.

MISSION
To empower Latinx families, providing a foundation for systemic change with long-term
effects. La Casita uses a ground-up strategy to help the community access services and
overcome barriers.

MODEL
The "accompaniment model" — an evidence-based practice focused on empowering individuals
to make changes in their own lives. La Casita uses accompaniment to address systemic
issues through community care that is trauma-informed and rooted in equity and
accessibility. Holistic approach for the whole person/family.

IMPACT (2025 Annual Report figures)
- 76,024 services provided
- 1,211 outgoing referrals
- 3,705 individuals accompanied
- 2,563 families accompanied
- 8,161 phone calls handled
- 99 unique zip codes served
- 44 countries of origin
- 23 languages spoken
